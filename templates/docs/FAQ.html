Frequently Asked Questions

Here we will expand on some questions we have received.


  • How does the reward share frequency work?

    The standard frequency is set to weekly, meaning that after 7 days you can expect a reward share. For more information, check this blog

  • Do you cover the voting and transaction fees?

    Yes we indeed cover the transaction fees for weekly and monthly payouts. If your payout is set to monthly you receive an additional bonus of two times the transaction fee.

  • If I have already voted, and then add more Ark to my wallet, will I need to vote again?

    No, once you have voted, that address is linked to the delegate. You can add or remove Ark from your address whenever you want. Our server will know and adjust accordingly.

  • Can I still obtain the early adopter status?

    If you have voted for us during the 100% payout period (from the 11th to the 24th of september) you will receive early adopter status. If you transfer your wallet to a ledger, you can send us a request to grant Early Adopter status to the new address

  • How much ark will I get when I vote for you?

    The community has developed many calculators, for example: Dafty

    The basic formula used is: ((‘Your ark’ / ‘Total Ark voted for delegate’)* ‘share percentage’) * 422)

  • Why does the share on the calculator differ every day?

    That has to do with the ‘Total Ark voted for delegate’. This changes every day (or even more often) when people switch votes around.

  • Is there a minimum share?

    For daily the minimum share is 2 ark, for weekly 0.1 Ark and for monthly 0.01 Ark. If your share is lower than the minimum, you will receive a share as soon it crosses the threshold
